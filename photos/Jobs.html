FAQ


Slice Order Policy - Effective July 27th, 2015.

Slices are no longer available for delivery starting July 27th, 2015.
Instead, we now offer two additional sizes of pies. All of our pizzas are available in 18", 16", and 12" sizes.


Future Orders:

We welcome you to call days, weeks, or even months in advance to order Mesa Pizza pies
for your meeting or party, because it's good to plan ahead.
Mesa Pizza looks forward to catering your next event!
We can provide plates and napkins if you need them, just ask us!
We also offer a big group salad, which serves roughly 10 people. The big salad is $30 and needs to be ordered at least a day in advance.


How many people does one of your pizzas feed?

Good question, although it is hard to give a definite answer because we donâ€™t know how hungry you are. An 18" pizza (18" diameter)
typically feeds 5-6 people (or 4 to 5 college students). A 16" pizza typically feeds 3-4 people, and a 12" pizza typically feeds 1 to 3 people.


How many slices do you cut the pizza into?

We automatically cut 18" and 16" pizzas into 8 slices, but if you would like us to cut it into 16 slices or squares, we can do that too. We cut 12" pizzas into 6 slices.


Do you accept checks?

We DO NOT accept personal checks, but we take business or organizational checks. Checks from the University, Fraternities, Sororities,
Non-Profit groups, and Companies are accepted.


Do you have Gift Cards?

Yes! We have the credit-card-style gift cards that you can load up for any amount. Give one to your friends as a Birthday or Holiday gift,
or just because you like them! Additionally, we can mail it to you if you live far away. Please keep in mind that these gift cards are store specific,
in other words, Dinkytown gift cards work only at Dinkytown, Uptown gift cards work only at Uptown, etc.


I need a copy of my receipt, what should I do?

If you need a copy of your receipt, be sure to grab your copy from the driver when they drop off your pizza, they will always have a copy available for you.
If you forget to do that, call Mesa during the afternoon, and we can look you up and print you another copy.
When you call, be sure to have the date of the delivery, along with the last 4 digits of the credit card number
used to pay for the pizza.


Why won't you deliver to me? I live just outside of your delivery zone!

We understand your frustration, but unfortunately we have to set the boundary somewhere. You can always order a pizza for Pick-Up, and it will be delicious!
